About us

Who am I?

Hi!! I'm Mr. Bot, your virtual assistant. It is important to provide clinical guidance to patients according to their symptoms. Before going to the doctor, many people search for their symptoms via the internet and misdiagnose themselves. Mr. Bot aims to guide patients to clinical services by checking symptoms automatically and to acquire required healthcare services quickly. Patients can get information about the possible disease (s) and related clinical service (s) by telling their symptoms to the Mr. Bot. Mr. Bot provides 24x7 assistance but Mr. Bot cannot substitute professional healthcare consulting and a visit to a doctor is a must.
